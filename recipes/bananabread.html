Banana Bread

Description

Healthy low sugar but delicious banana bread

  • 100 gr Oat flour
  • 50gr Espelta flour
  • 50gr whole flourn
  • 4 Eggs
  • 30gr sugar
  • 3 Bananas
  • 30gr of vegan vanilla flavour protein powder
  • Cinemon
  • 35gr Butter
  • 50gr Nuts
  • 20gr of baking powder

Steps

  1. Mix well the eggs and sugar
  2. Add the flours and protein powder
  3. Smash the bananas with the butter and the cinemon and add to the mixture
  4. Mix everything well, until a uniform mass
  5. Add the baking powder and mix a bit more
  6. Take to the hoven for 40 minutes in a 180C degrees
